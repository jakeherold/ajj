Section 1:

Our group , is propopsing to create a road trip calculator for our final project, that will tell users distance, route, amount of gas needed and approximate cost of their trip. Our initial user stories are as follows:

As a user:

  • I want to quickly be able to see my:
      Miles
      Gas
      Cost
      Time
  • I want easy to use but advanced filter options
  • I want my information to persist when I return (cached)
  • I want a visual represantation of my trip and info
